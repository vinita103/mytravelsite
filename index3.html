The Resort

We stayed at the brightly coloured, all-inclusive Royal Decameron Cornwall Beach in Montego Bay. Very Caribbean, relaxed kinds of vibes fill the resort—exactly the kind of ambience you want when you’re vacationing in Jamaica. The resort has an awesome pool and is beachfront. Plus, you can rent canoes and paddle boards on the beach if you’re looking for some fun day activities
